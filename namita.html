Namita

2016 - 2018
DPS

"Babita Ma’am has been a very supportive teacher. She understood that we need to study other subjects or exams and would considerably give us homework. She taught me science in 9th and 10th grade but I was free to ask my doubts related to maths as well. I remember and thank her for all the extra classes she took. I remember how talkative I was and how she would give us time to laugh on my PJs and then continue the lecture. It was truly a fun and engaging class.

I have heard teachers say “you can ask me the same question 10 times and I will be happy helping you out.” Babita Ma’am genuinely lives by it! Undoubtedly my batchmates and I have asked the same question, the same experiments n number of times and she would always explain us with utmost dedication and peace. I scored 90% in science in my 10th Board exams but trust me if you are a tad more dedicated than I was in my 10th grade, you can score what ever score you wish to!

Now ma’am and I share recipes, talk about life in general and this just proves how good of a person and teacher she is, that her students want to stay in touch with her even after passing out :)"
