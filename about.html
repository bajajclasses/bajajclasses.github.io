About Me

"Teaching is to make a difference in someone's life"

I am Ms. Babita Bajaj . I am into teaching profession for more than past 20 years . I strive to work with students to find out how they learn best and implement the best strategies possible to enrich their interests and needs. I enjoy using my knowledge to help them succeed . Using my unique teaching methods I am currently teaching Science and Maths to students of classes 8-10.


My Teaching Philosophy


My teaching philosophy is to enable students to learn, understand and explore the subject beyond the textbooks. I believe that hard work and determination have a lifelong impact on shaping confidence and achieving your goals. I try to simplify the complex concepts so that it is easily understood by students.


My Approach


My approach towards teaching is to allow students to feel comfortable to ask questions and show their understanding of the content. I believe that making learning fun is an important part in helping students to develop passion for learning. I try to help build the students' confidence by encouraging them to do their best.
